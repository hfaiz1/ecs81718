D1-Now-Future

This section of my website talks about from the time I was born to where I'm at now to where I want to be in a couple years. My life started September 19, 2002 in Casablanca, Morocco. A healthy 9 pound baby blessed to a recently married couple. I was raised in Casablanca until I was three or four which is when I moved to America with my parents. When i moved to America, I lived in a small town called Moline,IL and that was where I was raised up until fifth grade. In fifth grade, I moved to Chicago and went to Dirksen Elementary. After elementary school, I got into Lane Tech and that's where I go now. My plans for the future is to get into law and become a lawyer. I also want to start my own business in a couple years. I want to start a clothing line for women who wear the scarf and be able to sell long dresses, skirts, scarves, etc.
