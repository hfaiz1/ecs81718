What Am I?

I am originally from Casabalnca, Morocco which would make me Moroccan. Morocco is a North African country that borders the Atlantic ocean and the Mediterranean Sea and is next to Algeria and across the Mediterranean you would find Spain. There are also two spanish territories on Moroccan Soil called Ceuta and Melilla. The country is filled with Berber, European, and Arab influence whether it's the buildings or historical sits or social norms. Morocco won its independence from France on November 18th 1956. Morocco was under European rule as well as Arab rule for long period of times. That is why there are multiple skin colors, hair colors, and eye colors in Morocco and these influences helped shape the moroccan dialect. Personally I know that I am Berber and European on my dad's side of the family and Arab, Berber, and European on my mom's side of the family. Berbers are the indigenous people of Morocco. Some of them live very old fashioned lives and have their own language which my family members on my dad's side speak. My European roots are farther down the line on both sides and I have minimal or almost no Arab roots on my Dad's side. My berber roots on my mom's side are also farther down the line. Overall I consider myself Moroccan so when a person approaches me and asks what I am, I reply with "I am Moroccan."
