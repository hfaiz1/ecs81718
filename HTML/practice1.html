Linda Sarsour

She does not have a nickname


Linda Sarsour also stands up for Palestinian rights and the rights of Muslims in America.

She works with other politicians and activists to get a spot in the table and make social and political problems aware to people who can introduce change to society.

Learn More About Linda Sarsour

  • Volunteered at Arab American Association of New York
  • Helped organize the Arab Muslim community's response to the Black Lives Matter protests
  • Spoke as a surrogate for U.S Senator, Bernie Sanders
  • She was co-chairwoman of the 2017 Day without a woman strike and protest
  • She was arrested during a demonstration outside Trump's hotel.
